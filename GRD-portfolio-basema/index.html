ATELIER FASHION SHOW

For the 2024 Atelier Fashion Show and the 10th anniversary, my design was chosen for the St. Clair College students’ fashion show. It was exciting to see my design featured on screens, social media, prints, and around campus.

Inspired by the 90s theme, I created a flat illustration of a 90s-style model using vibrant colors, fabric textures, sharp triangle shapes, and black strokes that evoke a sense of fun and energy.
